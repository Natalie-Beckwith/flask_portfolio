Food Court


The food court is a place where students go to eat during break and lunch. This area is made up of two parts, the lunch tables and the building where students get the food. Common dishes include Bean and Cheese Burrito, Cheese or Pepperoni Pizza, Cheeseburger, and Turkey Sandwiches.
