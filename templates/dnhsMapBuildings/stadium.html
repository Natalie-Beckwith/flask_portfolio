The Stadium


About the Stadium

The Stadium at Del Norte is one of the most visited sites on campus, having the capacity to seat thousands of nighthawks as well as visitors from other schools. The primary function of the stadium is to house football games, but the field and surrounding track are also used for other games and sports.

About the teams

Multiple different sports teams practice and play at this very stadium, including football, lacrosse, track & field, and others. With football being the most popular sport in the school, there are usually home games held at the stadium on Fridays that are huge events for everybody participating.

Other Events

Aside from just sports, the stadium is also used for evacuations and rallies. In the case of a hazardous natural disaster, it is protocol to evacuate to the field in the stadium. Along with this, orientation for a new year of school is held here as well as some other large events.
