The Tennis Courts


About the Tennis Courts

The tennis courts at Del Norte are the home of many nighthawk victories. Both the Del Norte girls and boys tennis teams use these courts as well as students taking ENS. The area comprises of 6 total tennis courts, which can be used for tennis as well as some other net-based games.

About the teams

Del Norte has 2 different Tennis teams that actively use this court. The girls tennis team plays between August and November. The boys team plays between February and June. The coach for both the boys and girls tennis team is Mrs. Cherise Meoli, who received her tennis scholarship at Long Beach State and has since been teaching tennis at Del Norte since 2011.
